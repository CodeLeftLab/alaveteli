500 MOD Horses

Joe Admin made this Freedom of Information request to Ministry of Defence

The request was partially successful.

From: Joe Admin

7 August 2013

Dear Ministry of Defence,

It has recently been reported that the MOD has more than 500
horses.

Please provide the most recent annual figures available for the
following:

1. Feeding costs for all MOD horses;

2. Veterinary costs for all MOD horses;

3. Stabling costs for all MOD horses;

4. Farrier costs for all MOD horses;

5. Tack and horse wear costs for all MOD horses;

6. Grooming costs for all MOD horses;

7. Transport costs associated with the movement of all MOD horses;

8. All other costs associated with MOD horses.

Yours faithfully,

Joe Admin
